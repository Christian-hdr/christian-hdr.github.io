Who are we?

Welcome to Smoking Beards. Here you will find the finest selection of uniquely hand crafted spice mixes designed to up your grilling game in your own backyard. Me? I'm just a regular guy, fueled by an undying and long standing passion for cooking, grilling and smoking. And I created Smoking Beards to share my fire with fellow barbecue enthusiasts, amateur pitmasters and professional chefs, so pick your rub, light your fire, and enjoy the ride!

The Original

Initially developed in our lab for the Smoke'n'Flames competition, "The Original" is the first of our signature spice mixes. Designed with rich meats in mind, it is spectacular on briskets, but its unique flavor profile makes it an all-rounder that works well with almost every meats and dairy alike!
